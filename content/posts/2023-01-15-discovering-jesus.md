John (the disciple) records John the Baptist's confession of Jesus as the Lamb of God. When two of John's disciples hear this confession, they follow Jesus. After spending time with Jesus, Andrew brings his brother (Simon) to Jesus. As we consider Jesus, may we follow him and invite others to do the same.
