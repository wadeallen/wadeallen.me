In John 13, Jesus knows his time on earth is coming to an end. He is preparing for the Cross. John chooses to highlight an event at the Last Supper that catches the disciples off guard, especially Peter. Jesus lowers himself to perform the menial task of washing His disciples feet. In this lesson, the disciples are urged to take on the same posture toward one another. As followers of Jesus, we must carefully consider our posture toward one another.
