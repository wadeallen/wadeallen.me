Moses travels to Egypt in obedience to God. When He arrives he is well received by the Israelite elders. They believe and worship. But his first encounter with Pharaoh is not all Moses hoped it would be. He becomes discouraged and doubtful. As we consider the ups and downs of the Christian life, we are assured of God's continued presence and provision in the uncertainties of following Him.
