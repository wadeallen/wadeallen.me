As we begin our sermon series on prayer, we find Jesus teaching His disciples to pray in two gospel accounts (Matthew and Luke). Praying is not always easy; it is sometimes difficult to know what to say. This teaching helps us understand that Jesus is not offering us magical words to summon God. Rather, these words help us gain the appropriate posture in coming to God.
