In the Lord's Prayer, we pray for God's Kingdom to come and His will be done on earth as it is in heaven. As we explore both the Jewish understanding of the Kingdom of God and Jesus' teaching on the Kingdom of God, we are able to better understand this prayer. Might we be willing for God's rule and reign in our lives, in our church, in our community, and in our world.
