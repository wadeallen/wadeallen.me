In our stewardship emphasis campaign (4 Sundays), we will be exploring parables about resources. Parables are the way that Jesus taught about the Kingdom of God. He used these stories to help us understand the values of His Kingdom. The Rich Man and Lazarus reminds us that Kingdom Living does not allow us to hold tightly to our riches while others are suffering. As we encounter this story, we are reminded of God's desire for us to share all He has given to us.
