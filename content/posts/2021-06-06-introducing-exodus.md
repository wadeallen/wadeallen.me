In our first week in our series over the book of Exodus, we explore key themes in this story. In the first few chapters, the scene is set with Pharaoh oppressing the people of God. At the same time, God is working and moving, preparing to rescue His people. In the same way, God continues to work and move in the midst of our dark days.
