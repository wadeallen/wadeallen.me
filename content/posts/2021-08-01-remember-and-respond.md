As the people of God are delivered from the grip of Pharaoh, God calls His people to remember and respond. They are summoned to set up celebrations to remember God's saving acts. At the same time, God reminds His people that they belong to Him. From generation to generation, God's actions must be remembered and renewed in the lives of His people.
