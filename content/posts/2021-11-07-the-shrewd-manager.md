In the parable of the shrewd manager, Jesus surprises us. He commends the dishonest manager for his ability to assess his situation, plan for the future, and give the energy to make provisions for himself when he is without a job. As we live in the Kingdom of God, we must have the same posture in how we use the resources given to us by God.
