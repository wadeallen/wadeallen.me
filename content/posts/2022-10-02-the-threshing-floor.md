Naomi’s concern for Ruth’s welfare was evident as she urged her to return to her family. In chapter 3, Naomi devises a plan for Ruth’s rescue. Ruth agrees to follow Naomi’s plan but steps it up a level by calling on Boaz to act as kinsman-redeemer. The tension of the story builds in this late night interaction between Ruth and Boaz. Ruth’s courage and willingness to risk is a model for our following Jesus. She trusts God as she steps out in faith.
