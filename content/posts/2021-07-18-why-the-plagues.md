As we continue the story of the Exodus, we find Moses discouraged following Pharaoh's refusal to release the Israelites. However, God assures Moses that He will deliver His people. In chapters 7-10, we find a series of plagues that serve to convince Pharaoh to release the enslaved Israelites. As we study these chapters, we can be assured of God's continued persistence in continuing His work of redemption and renewal today.
