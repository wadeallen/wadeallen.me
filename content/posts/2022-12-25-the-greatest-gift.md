On Christmas morning we gather (one service at 11 AM) to reflect on the one true gift, Jesus. We consider the word of Isaiah hundreds of years before Jesus was born. He reveals all that Jesus came to accomplish. As we reflect on his description, we understand the superlative nature of all that Jesus brings to us.
