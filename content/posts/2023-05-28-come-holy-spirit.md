On Pentecost Sunday, we explore the pouring out of God's Spirit in Numbers 11. In the midst of complaining and  disgruntled people, Moses is discouraged and ready to give up. God chooses to expand the work of the Spirit by including additional leaders in an outpouring of His Spirit. As we consider our day and time, may God pour out his Spirit on our church and community.
