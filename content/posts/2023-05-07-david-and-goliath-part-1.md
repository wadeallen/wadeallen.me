This is one of the most familiar stories in all of Scripture. David battles Goliath. Against all odds, a young boy faces an experienced and heavily resourced giant. We will cover this story the next two weeks. In the first week, we will consider David's reliance on God. David is sure that God will provide all he needs for the challenge.
