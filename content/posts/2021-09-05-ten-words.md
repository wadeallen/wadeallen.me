The 10 Commandments are one of the most familiar passages in the Bible. But we often see this list of rules as an obligation that limits our freedom. Instead, might we understand them as a gift from a loving and rescuing God. As we live as God intends, we will find the freedom that comes from living in sync with a holy God.
