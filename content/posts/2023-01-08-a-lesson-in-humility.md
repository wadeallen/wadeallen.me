Jesus described John the Baptist as the greatest man every born. He was faithful to God's calling, living in the desert, eating locusts and honey, preaching a bold and offensive message. In fact, he would be killed for this valiant message. Yet when Jesus approaches him for baptism, John's initial response was to forbid Jesus. We will explore how John's tendency continues to be our own experience.
