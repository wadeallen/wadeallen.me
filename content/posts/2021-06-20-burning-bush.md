The people of God are suffering under Egyptian oppression. It seems like God has forgotten His people. All the while, God is preparing a rescue mission, and He is calling His servant, Moses, to lead the charge. In Exodus 3:1-10, Moses is called by God in a dramatic fashion. Might we consider God's calling in our lives as we study this passage together.
