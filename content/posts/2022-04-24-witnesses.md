Peter and the apostles are jailed for teaching about Jesus. They are miraculously released by an angel and instructed to continue to proclaim new life in Jesus. When challenged by the religious leaders, they refuse to be silenced. As Christians today, we too are called to share the message of Jesus' resurrection with the world around us.
