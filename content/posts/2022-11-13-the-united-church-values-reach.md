In our stewardship series, we focus on four values of The United Church (Worship, Grow, Care, Reach). We exist as a church to reach out. In Luke 10, Jesus sends out disciples into towns and villages. We, like these disciples, are sent out on mission for God. God is deeply concerned for the world around us. May we carefully discern God's work in the world around us and join Him in bringing the Kingdom of God to our community and our world.
