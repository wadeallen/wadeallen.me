In our stewardship series, we focus on four values of The United Church (Worship, Grow, Care, Reach). We exist as a church to care for one another. Paul instructs Christians to carry each other's burdens. This is more than simply doing nice deeds. It involves walking spiritually with one another, pointing toward Jesus and His way of living.
