When Moses encounters God on Mount Sinai, he is given instructions for the tabernacle. God is not confined to a smoke filled, fiery mountain. He desires to dwell among his people, set up camp with them. And the instructions for his dwelling place reminds us of his holiness and transformative power in the lives of His people.
