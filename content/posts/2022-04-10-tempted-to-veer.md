As we journey with Jesus to the Mount of Olives, we are reminded of our continual struggle to veer from the Father. Jesus' drops of blood in the garden remind us of the intensity of His battle to go to the Cross for us. At the same time, we hear Jesus' words to the disciples to pray that they will not fall into temptation. In a culture that diminishes the existence of sin, might we heed these instructions each day.
