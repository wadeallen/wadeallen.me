In Exodus 12, God sends the final blow, the knockout punch to Pharaoh. We will explore God's instructions to His people in killing the Passover Lamb, painting the blood on their door posts. As they follow God's instructions, they will be saved. This event describes the work of Jesus as the ultimate Passover Lamb.
