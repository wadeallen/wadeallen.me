As we celebrate the Risen Christ, we will explore John 20:1-10. Mary Magdalene finds and empty tomb, and darts off to find Peter and John. They race to the tomb to find strips of linen and a head cloth. John expresses belief in the Risen Christ without fully understanding the significance of what he sees. How do we respond to the empty tomb?
