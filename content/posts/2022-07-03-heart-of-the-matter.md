Pagans in the first century offered prayer to various deities. They would use big words and lengthy prose, attempting to impress their god and manipulate their gods to give them what they wanted. Jesus urges His followers to take a very different approach. God is our Father. He loves us and wants us to approach Him authentically, offering our simple requests to Him.
