As we continue to explore vignettes from the book of Acts, we find Paul and his team hindered in their plans. God leads them to an unexpected place where they encounter a group of women. As they relate the story of Jesus, God opens the heart of a key player in the establishment of the church in Philippi. God often thwarts our plans in order to accomplish His purposes.
