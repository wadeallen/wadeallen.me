On the Sunday after Christmas, we will explore the story of Jesus at twelve years old. He remains in the Temple following the Passover celebration. His parents don't realize He is missing until they are on their way home. This is one of the only stories we have of the boy Jesus. Let's consider what it means for us during this Christmas season.
