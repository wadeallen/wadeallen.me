In Exodus 30:11-16, Moses is instructed to conduct a census, a dangerous thing to do in the Old Testament. God's people are to step forward, offer a meager offering, and be counted. Each person is able to personally identify with God's work and mission. In the same way, we are given the opportunity to be a part of Jesus' life, death, and resurrection by believing in Him.
