In Peter's fourth sermon in Acts, Peter highlights the Risen Christ as Lord of all and the judge of the living and the dead. Peter explains to Cornelius how this Risen Christ provides the forgiveness of sins. The strange dynamics of Cornelius' story remind us of God's saving plan through the Risen Christ. As we celebrate the Resurrection of Jesus, we understand its significance in our lives today.
