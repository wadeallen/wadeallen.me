As we gather on Christmas Eve to worship and celebrate communion, we are reminded that Christmas is not simply a birthday party for a good man. Christmas is the celebration of God stepping into our world. It is as much about his work (His death on the Cross) as it is about his lying in a manger. Remembering his body broken for us and his blood shed for us is perhaps the most appropriate way to begin our Christmas celebrations.
