In our stewardship series, we focus on four values of The United Church (Worship, Grow, Care, Reach). We exist as a church to worship. Worship is not just what we do on Sunday mornings, although it includes our Sunday services. It happens in various ministries, visits, and personal times with the Lord. Worship centers on the Triune God who has revealed His Truth to us. It is about offering ourselves to God, empowered by the Holy Spirit.
