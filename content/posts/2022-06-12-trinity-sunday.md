As we gather on Trinity Sunday, we explore the doctrine of the Triune God, the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit. We explore what the Bible teaches us about the Trinity, the question of how we can make sense of it, and the implications of worshiping a Triune God. As a part of this study, we will highlight the elements of our building and our worship that point to God as three-in-one.
