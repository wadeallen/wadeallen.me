Ruth followed Naomi's plan as she met Boaz on the threshing floor. But she risked it all when she took the request to the level of kinsman-redeemer. Boaz would need to get legal clearance for this one. In Ruth 4, Boaz gathers the elders at the city gate. He pursues the redemption of Ruth and Naomi, even if it involves financial risk. Boaz steps out in faith, taking considerable risk, to redeem Ruth. Like Jesus, Boaz is selfless and puts Ruth's needs ahead of his own. May we live in this sort of selfless concern for others.
