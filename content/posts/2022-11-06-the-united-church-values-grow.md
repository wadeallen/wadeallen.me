In our stewardship series, we focus on four values of The United Church (Worship, Grow, Care, Reach). We exist as a church to grow. The author of Hebrews expresses frustration with the Christians in the first century. He describes their imaturity as a result of thier laziness and unwillingness to grow. As Christians, we are called to grow in our faith so that we fully understand God's calling in our lives.
