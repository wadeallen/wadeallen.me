As we conclude our series on the book of Exodus, we focus on the closing scene in this story. While God's presence was in question after the enormous failure of His people (Golden Calf episode), God reiterates His presence and expectations for His people. The final scene reveals God's presence (cloud and fire) hovering over the tabernacle. The people are constantly watching and waiting for His direction. Might we be a people who consistently look to God in everything we do.
