We continue to discuss the story of David and Goliath. Last week, we considered David's reliance on God as he faced Goliath. This week, we explore the battle itself. David approaches Goliath in the name of the Lord. He does not approach this challenge in his own power, but relies on God. As we consider challenges in our lives, God must become the center of our focus.
