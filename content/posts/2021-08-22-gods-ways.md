We circle back around the narratives in Exodus 13-18 to discuss a theology of hardship and suffering. When God calls Moses to lead the people of God out of Egypt, He never promises a journey free from trouble. Once God's people are free from Egypt, they encounter challenge after challenge. This is how God molds His people. The same is true for us today.
