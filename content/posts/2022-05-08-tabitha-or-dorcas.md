On Mother's Day, we consider the story of Tabitha/Dorcas. A widow who has contributed greatly to the needs of others, dies. Peter is summoned to the scene and raises her from the dead. Her life and resurrection are a model for selflessly serving others. As we honor women on this day, let us consider the example of New Testament women like Tabitha/Dorcas.
