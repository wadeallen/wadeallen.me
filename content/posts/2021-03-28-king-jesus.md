On Palm Sunday, we reflect on Jesus' entry into Jerusalem on a donkey. Some were true followers who had seen Jesus' works and believed in Him. Others had only heard of Jesus, his miracles, and came to check Him out. Others resisted Jesus' claim to be the Messiah, and sought to kill Him.
