In the second part of the story of Jesus' return to his hometown, the townspeople attempt to kill Jesus. Jesus relates the stories of the widow at Zarephath and Naaman the Leper to warn them of the consequences of failing to trust God. As we encounter the story of Jesus, what is our response? Are we willing to accept and believe, to trust Him and submit to Him?
