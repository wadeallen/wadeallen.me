It has been over a year since my last post. Ministry has a way of filling the calendar, but I am feeling a nudge to return.

There is something about writing that brings clarity. As I put words on the screen, it helps me reflect on what I am observing, wrestling with, and learning in ministry. I have missed that.

I am not sure how often I will write. We live in a world of too many words. But this blog has always been less about the audience and more about the discipline. It is about the slow work of paying attention. So here we go again.
